# Content Guide

## Honest Positioning

Use clear language that presents this as a planning dashboard and portfolio project. Do not imply the app can launch, optimize, or manage real campaigns.

Approved framing:

- "Portfolio campaign planning dashboard"
- "Planning only, no ad account connection"
- "Not affiliated with Google, Meta, or any ads platform"
- "Campaign planning dashboard by Fazil Waseem"

Avoid:

- "Launch campaigns directly"
- "Guaranteed results"
- "Official Google Ads tool"
- "Official Meta Ads tool"
- "Automated media buying platform"

## Dashboard Sections

### Business Brief

Collects:

- Business name
- Website or landing page
- Offer
- Business summary
- Monthly budget
- Target location

### Campaign Objective

Supported objective types:

- Lead generation
- Ecommerce
- Service business

Each objective should influence:

- Primary KPI
- Funnel direction
- Budget split
- Keyword ideas
- Audience segments

### Google Ads Planning

Use real workflow concepts:

- Search campaign structure
- Keyword intent
- Landing page match
- Remarketing
- Conversion tracking readiness

### Meta Ads Planning

Use real workflow concepts:

- Prospecting
- Creative angles
- Objective selection
- Retargeting
- Offer reminders

### Target Audience

Encourage notes around:

- Audience traits
- Buying triggers
- Objections
- Exclusions
- Location and intent

### Keyword Ideas

Keep keyword ideas generic enough for a template but grounded in campaign intent.

### Ad Copy Planner

Collect:

- Headline
- Primary text
- Call to action

### Budget Planner

Show estimated allocation between:

- Google Ads
- Meta Ads
- Testing reserve

### Funnel Checklist

Cover:

- Offer clarity
- Landing page match
- Conversion action
- Follow-up process

### Launch Checklist

Cover:

- Objective selection
- Tracking and UTMs
- Ad review
- Budget confirmation

### Reporting/KPI Dashboard

Include metrics such as:

- CTR
- CPA
- CVR
- ROAS

Use planning questions instead of performance claims.
